# Local Parks — Pod Lead Take-Home

Thanks for investing your time with us. This exercise is deliberately open-ended. You are not required to write any code by hand.

## Time expectation

Budget **90 minutes** of focused effort. We calibrate our review to that, so scope accordingly.

## The scenario

A city's leadership has allocated budget for a digital initiative "to get residents outside and into our local parks." That sentence is the entire mandate — there is no spec, no research, and no PM above you. You own turning it into working software.

Pick any real or invented city. Use any data you like; invented or generated data is fine.

## The assignment

1. Write **`BRIEF.md`** (max 300 words) — your product brief for v1.
2. Write the markdown doc(s) you would use to direct an AI agent to build that v1.
3. Using whatever AI tools you normally reach for, build a working prototype a resident could click through.

## What to submit

Zip and send:

1. `BRIEF.md`
2. Your build doc(s) — the actual file(s) you fed the AI
3. The prototype, with a brief how-to-run note (or a working link)
4. Your full, uncurated AI transcript(s) — export the raw session logs from your tool(s)

## The follow-up conversation (~45 min)

After you submit, we'll schedule a call where you demo the prototype and we discuss your submission together.

Questions are welcome, but where the prompt is silent, use your judgment.
